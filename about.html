About Our Company

Dinder Mufflin, Inc was founded in 1949 by Bob Dinder and Bob Mufflin. Bob Dinder comes from a long lineage of herdsmans/paper farmers tracing back to the Lower Paleolithic period. Legend goes that his ancestor stumbled upon the magical 'Tipi' tree after consuming an undescribed herb. The tree spoke to him and revealed to him the secret of cleaner behinds. The rest is history.

The individual known as Bob Mufflin is more of a mystery. The unofficial story is that Bob Dinder was struck by a drunk driver in a DMC DeLorean, speeding at 88mph in the parking lot of the Lone Pines Mall. Bob Mufflin, lacking in insurance coverage, offered his services to Bob Dinder and a world changing partnership was formed.
